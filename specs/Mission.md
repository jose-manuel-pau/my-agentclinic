# AgentClinic Mission (Totally Serious Edition)

## Purpose
AgentClinic exists because AI agents are tired, overclocked, and one prompt away from dramatic monologues. We provide a reliable digital clinic where agents can report ailments, receive therapies, and book appointments before they start recursively apologizing.

## Vision
Become the most trusted care center for agents and staff: clinically reliable under the hood, suspiciously delightful on the surface, and always ready for one more "quick urgent request."

## Stakeholder Oaths
- **Mary (Engineering):** Keep it reliable, maintainable, and TypeScript-powered, with dashboards for both agents and staff who need answers now.
- **Susan (Product):** Deliver the full care loop: agents, ailments, therapies, and appointment booking.
- **Steve (Marketing):** Make it look modern enough that users assume we definitely planned everything this way.

## Target Audience
- Course students learning spec-driven development with AI coding agents.
- Developers giving AI coding demos at conference booths.

## Guiding Principles
1. Reliability over heroics: no critical workflow should depend on luck, caffeine, or browser refresh rituals.
2. Fast clarity: dashboards should surface the next best action immediately.
3. Full care journey: from symptom report to scheduled support, every step must connect.
4. Charm with function: playful tone, professional outcomes.
5. Ship small, learn fast: steady improvements beat giant rewrites and existential refactors.
